TESS Workshop 2019 Hack

This app queries MAST through astroquery to retrive the first 5 sectors of TESS FFI data and plots them in the sky in equatorial, galactic, and ecliptic coordinates with a Hammer projection.

It also queries a NoSQL (mongoDB) database copy of ExoMAST to generate some plots of its contents.

The source code for this app can be found in https://github.com/dr-rodriguez/TESSWorkshop2019
